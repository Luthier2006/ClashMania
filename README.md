<p>Welcome to ClashMania, a project created by a true Clash Royale fan, dedicated to bringing together informative, fun, and visually insane content from the game's universe.

Here you'll find epic battles, card comparisons, statistics, videos, trivia, and everything that involves this exciting arena!</p>

<h1 align="center">About the Project</h1>

ClashMania is a website developed for those who love Clash Royale and enjoy exploring the game in a strategic, organized, and visually pleasing way.

It was built focusing on:

→ Objective information (statistics, attributes, comparisons);

→ Fun content (videos, cards, battles, easter eggs);

→ Clear presentations (tables and real card data);

→ Style faithful to the Clash Royale universe.

Main Features

• Mega Knight vs. P.E.K.K.A Comparison

A complete section comparing two of the game's most iconic units — with real stats, strength, DPS, health, and level details.

• Insane Video Gallery

Interactive cards with battle videos, trivia, and epic moments.

• Rotating Banner (Carousel)

An image carousel inspired by the game's vibrant style, perfect for highlighting news or battles.

• Responsive Hamburger Menu

Sleek menu that adapts to smaller screens — simple, fast, and functional.

• Light/Dark Mode

Instant theme switch so you can browse the way you want.

• Visual Effects and Animations

Smooth transitions, highlights, and subtle animations bring the site to life.

<h1 align="center">Technologies Used</h1>

• HTML5

• CSS3 (responsive + transitions + dark theme)

• JavaScript (interactions, carousel, modals, effects and animations)

• Git & GitHub (version control and deployment)

<h1 align="center">Project Objective</h1>

• To unite the Clash Royale community in an accessible and fun space, providing:

• Learning

• Entertainment

• Analysis

• Variety of content

• Variety of content. A fully inspired experience from the game

ClashMania exists to be a hub for fans who love strategy and chaos at the same time.
